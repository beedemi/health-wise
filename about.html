A Few Words About Us

An Health Organisation for Almost 0 Years

Health Wise is dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of Nigerians by harnessing the power of stories, rigorous research methods, and the internet to understand and share experiences of: what it’s really like to have a particular health condition or to care for someone with that condition, and what is and isn't working in our health and social care system.

Our online resources provide support and information to other patients and carers, and are a resource for health professionals and health educators. In combination with our academic outputs, they are also a source of evidence for service providers, policymakers and researchers about what is and isn’t working in the Nigeriann healthcare system. A commitment to knowledge translation, research independence, research excellence, and respect for research participants informs our work.

Mission:

Mission Health Wise is a group of academic researchers dedicated to improving the health of Nigerian by harnessing the power of patient / service user, carer and health professional stories. Our mission is to use the power of individual stories to understand and improve health and social care for all Nigerians, placing the person at the centre stage of health and social care improvement and reform.

Aims

Conduct research using the methodology developed by HERG - University of Oxford and DIPEx UK, thus ensuring a high quality evidence-based approach to personal experiences of health and illness.

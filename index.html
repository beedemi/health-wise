Our Mission

Mission Health Wise is a group of academic researchers dedicated to improving the health of Nigerian by harnessing the power of patient / service user, carer and health professional stories. Our mission is to use the power of individual stories to understand and improve health and social care for all Nigerians, placing the person at the centre stage of health and social care improvement and reform.
